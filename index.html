About Us

International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience (IAESTE) Established as a global non-governmental and non-profit organization, the International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience (IAESTE) is dedicated to facilitating mutually beneficial partnerships between students, employers, and universities through its esteemed internship program.

History of IAESTE

In 1948, Mr. James Newby led 10 European nations in establishing an international youth exchange program. The initiative, founded on the belief that globally experienced students are key to addressing modern challenges, aimed to foster cross-cultural competence. Today, this visionary effort's legacy underscores the ongoing significance of international experiences in shaping future global leaders.

Global Oppurtunities in IAESTE

Operating in 80+ countries, IAESTE provides a gateway to diverse international experiences. Join our global community for a journey that expands your horizons both professionally and personally.

Our Mission

IAESTE is committed to fostering international collaboration and professional development by providing students with valuable opportunities to gain practical experience and cross-cultural skills through paid internships abroad. Simultaneously, we offer employers and universities access to a pool of talented international interns who bring fresh perspectives and insights to their respective industries and research endeavors.
